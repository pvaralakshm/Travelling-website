Famous Places in jammu and Kashmir


Vaishno Devi is visited for its religious significance, as it's a revered Hindu pilgrimage site dedicated to Goddess Durga. People visit to seek blessings and pay their respects, believing it's the sacred abode of a form of Goddess Durga. Beyond the temple itself, the trek to the shrine, the surrounding natural beauty, and other attractions like Bhairavnath Temple and Ardhkuwari Cave, add to the overall experience.
Srinagar, the jewel in the crown of Jammu and Kashmir, is a city that whispers tales of timeless beauty and resilience. As the summer capital of this northernmost Indian state, it paints a mesmerizing picture where nature's grandeur meets centuries of rich culture. Imagine waking up to mist-kissed mornings on the shores of the iconic Dal Lake, where shikaras glide gracefully, their reflections dancing on the mirror-like waters. As the day unfolds, you'll find yourself wandering through a living canvas - saffron-hued sunsets drape the sky, while vibrant gardens burst with colors that seem almost otherworldly.

Hajan Valley, also known as Betaab Valley, is a popular tourist destination in Pahalgam, Kashmir, known for its stunning natural beauty and cinematic fame. It's a scenic valley with lush meadows, snow-capped mountains, and the Lidder River. The valley was named after the Bollywood movie "Betaab," which was filmed there. Visiting Hajan Valley is a great way to experience the tranquility of the Himalayas, enjoy scenic views, and take beautiful photographs.
Gulmarg literally means “Meadow of flowers”. Gulmarg is located 60 KMs away from Srinagar which is an hour and half journey by car. Gulmarg has attracted millions of tourists throughout its existence with the scenic beauty of the Himalayan Mountains in the back drop. One of the main attractions in Gulmarg is the Gulmarg Gondola, the Highest Cable Car in the world. The 1st phase of the Gondola takes tourists to the height of 8530 ft to Kongdori Station and the second stage of the Gondola goes up to the height of 12293 ft
