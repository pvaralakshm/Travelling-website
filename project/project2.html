Famous Places in Araku


Araku Valley offers a variety of attractions including natural beauty, cultural experiences, and unique geological features. Key highlights include Borra Caves, Padmapuram Botanical Gardens, Chaparai Waterfalls, and the Tribal Museum. For those interested in nature, Ananthagiri Hills and the numerous waterfalls are must-sees.
The World of coffee Museum is a museum of the future: width a lively way of exhibiting and displaying, and an exhibition space that can interact with visitors. We provide visitors an unique experience that requires using all five senses (sound, sight, taste, smell, and touch).

Katiki Waterfalls is a popular destination in Araku Valley, offering a blend of adventure, relaxation, and natural beauty. It's known for its scenic beauty, the adventurous trek to reach the falls, and the chance to experience the tranquility of the Eastern Ghats.
Borra Caves are a popular tourist attraction near Visakhapatnam, Andhra Pradesh, India, known for their unique geological formations and the natural beauty of the surrounding area. The caves feature stunning stalactites and stalagmites, including formations resembling Shiva-Parvati, Mother-Child, and other figures. The caves are part of a larger region known for its lush coffee plantations, scenic landscapes, and comfortable weather, making it a great destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.
